# HeyPearl Image Prompts

Operational intelligence, elegant systems, modern infrastructure. Prompts are tuned for Midjourney, DALL·E, Flux, and Ideogram. Every image should sit inside the same warm, editorial, lifestyle-tech world that represents elegant operational leverage.

## Brand context

P.E.A.R.L. stands for **Process · Efficiency · Automation · Revenue · Leverage**. It is a strategic framework, not a literal pearl. None of the visuals should reference pearls, oysters, jewelry, ocean, or beach imagery.

The visual system represents:
- operational intelligence
- intelligent workflows
- modern infrastructure
- layered process visuals
- warm AI-powered business operations
- premium startup / editorial energy

## Master style note (use as a suffix on any prompt below if needed)

> Editorial luxury startup aesthetic, warm cinematic lighting, cream and lavender base palette with magenta and orange accents, deep plum and navy shadows, premium magazine photography feel, spacious composition, shallow depth of field, soft natural daylight, subtle AI-era operational atmosphere, no text, no logos, no watermarks, no neon, no cyberpunk, no robotic imagery, no pearls, no oysters, no jewelry, no ocean, no beach, no underwater, no generic stock vibe.

Aspect ratio shorthand:

- 16:9 → 1920×1080
- 4:5 → 1280×1600 (portrait)
- 1:1 → 1200×1200
- 1.91:1 → 1200×630 (OG)
- 3:2 → 1500×1000

## 18. Common negatives to include in any prompt

> --no neon, --no cyberpunk, --no robot, --no humanoid AI, --no glitch, --no holographic interface clichés, --no motherboards, --no "matrix" digital rain, --no overdone gradients, --no hard primary blues, --no stock-photo people, --no fake brand logos, --no readable text on screens, --no watermarks, --no pearls, --no oysters, --no jewelry, --no ocean, --no underwater, --no beach.

If using Midjourney, append `--ar` to match aspect ratio, `--style raw --stylize 250` for editorial photography prompts, and `--stylize 400` for illustrative composition prompts.
